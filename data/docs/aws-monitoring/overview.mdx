## Overview

SigNoz provides comprehensive monitoring for your AWS infrastructure, collecting metrics, logs, and traces from various AWS services. You can choose between two integration approaches based on your needs.

## Integration Methods

You can choose between [**One-Click Integration**](https://signoz.io/docs/integrations/aws/one-click-aws-integrations/) (recommended for ease of use) and [**Manual Setup**](https://signoz.io/docs/aws-monitoring/one-click-vs-manual/#manual-setup-opentelemetry) (OpenTelemetry).

Not sure which to choose? See the [detailed comparison](https://signoz.io/docs/aws-monitoring/one-click-vs-manual/).
