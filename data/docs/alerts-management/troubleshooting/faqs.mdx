### Q. How can I setup synthetic monitoring checks/alerts with SigNoz?
A. Currently synthetic monitoring is not supported in SigNoz. But you can use httpcheck to create HTTP-based health checks for your services. More details [here](/docs/monitor-http-endpoints/).

### Q. Can I edit the subject of the alerts?

<details>

<summary> Toggle for answer </summary>

Yes, you can edit the subject of alerts for Slack, MS Teams, PagerDuty, and Opsgenie notification channels.

To edit the alert subject:

1. Navigate to **Settings**
2. Go to **Account Settings**
3. Select **Notification channel**
4. Click on the desired notification channel
5. Edit the **Title** field

You can also refer to the [Prometheus Alertmanager notifications guide](https://prometheus.io/docs/alerting/latest/notifications/) for more details on customizing alert templates.

**Note:** You cannot edit the subject of email alerts.

</details>
